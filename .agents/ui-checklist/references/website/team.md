# Team Checklist

- [ ] **Personality and branding** — Theme/tone that conveys how serious or fun your team is.
- [ ] **Team members** — Representation of your team, scaled by team size.
- [ ] **Visuals** — Photography or illustrations of team, offices, or culture.
- [ ] **Group by area or team** — Divide members by department for easier scanning.
- [ ] **Link to careers** — Connect team page to job openings.
