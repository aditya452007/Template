# Careers Checklist

- [ ] **Company story** — How the business started, the why and motivations.
- [ ] **Up to date information** — Especially quantitative values (headcount, offices, time operating).
- [ ] **Employee benefits** — Tangible value props for potential employees.
- [ ] **Teams and divisions** — Team breakdown scaled by company size.
- [ ] **Job openings** — All current roles, broken by department.
- [ ] **Working locations** — Office locations and remote work options.
- [ ] **Employee testimonials** — Stories from current employees.
