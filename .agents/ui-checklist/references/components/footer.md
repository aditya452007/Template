# Footer Checklist

- [ ] **Size and responsiveness** — Full width or contained.
- [ ] **Style** — Colours, font sizes, visual brand elements. Contrast with body content.
- [ ] **Content links** — Visuals, states, and destinations of all links.
- [ ] **Legal and compliance links** — Privacy policy, terms, legal content.
- [ ] **Logo or name of business** — Brand consistency, homepage link. Optional tagline.
- [ ] **Hierarchy and layout** — Balanced between logo, links, and actions. Group links into meaningful categories.
- [ ] **Social media** — Logos/links to active social platforms.
- [ ] **Mobile app download actions** — Apple/Google store links.
- [ ] **Trust/security badges** — Certifications and security measures.
