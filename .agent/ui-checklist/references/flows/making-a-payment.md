# Making a Payment Flow Checklist

- [ ] **Offer payment methods** — Credit/debit card with other third-party options.
- [ ] **Request card details** — Relevant text fields sized appropriately. Support saved payment methods.
- [ ] **Submit payment with card details entered** — Validate before submission. Handle error states (mistyped numbers, expired cards).
- [ ] **Show payment processing** — Loading page/indicator during processing.
- [ ] **Confirmation payment processed successfully** — Clear success indicator.
- [ ] **Outline next steps** — What happens next (order in motion, link to product).
