# Carousel Checklist

- [ ] **Ways of interaction** — Auto-play, manual navigation, or both.
- [ ] **Visual progress indicator** — Dots, numbers, or progress bar. Current slide should look distinct.
- [ ] **Dimensions and alignment** — Size and responsiveness of items. Content fills space without stretching.
- [ ] **Animation behaviour** — Direction, speed, and feel of transitions.
- [ ] **Focused item state** — Non-focused items differ in size, transparency, or colour.
