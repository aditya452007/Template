# Contact Us Checklist

- [ ] **Personality and branding** — Visual character and tone of voice aligned with brand.
- [ ] **Clear methods to contact** — Phone, email, or form.
- [ ] **Social media accounts** — Alternative contact via social platforms.
- [ ] **Segmenting contact methods** — Different contacts for support vs sales.
- [ ] **Easy location to get to** — Available in header or footer.
