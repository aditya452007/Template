# Color Theory — Rangon Ka Science

> Source: Figma "100 Color Combinations" + "Color Theory" articles

## Core Concepts

- **Color harmony** = visual balance jo design ko pleasing banata hai
- **Color impacts:** UX hierarchy, brand identity, mood, accessibility — sab kuch
- **Half of customers choose brands based primarily on color** (Adobe 2025)
- **Color is encoded 74% faster than text** — imagery ka power

## 7 Color Schemes

1. **Monochromatic** — ek color ke alag shades (soothing, clean)
2. **Analogous** — 3 adjacent colors on wheel (harmonious, brand-friendly)
3. **Complementary** — opposite colors (high contrast, attention-grabbing)
4. **Split-complementary** — base + 2 adjacent to opposite (balanced contrast)
5. **Triadic** — 3 evenly spaced (vibrant, playful)
6. **Tetradic (Double Complementary)** — 2 complementary pairs (rich, complex — expert use)
7. **Square** — 4 evenly spaced (balanced variety)

## Color Psychology for Your Startup

Tumhare startup ka vibe mix hai — to har section ke hisaab se:

- **Dashboard/Admin panels** — Monochromatic blue/grey (professional, focused)
- **Landing/Marketing pages** — Analogous + accent color (inviting, energetic)
- **CTA buttons** — Complementary accent (stands out)
- **Pricing section** — Blue + subtle green (trust + growth)
- **AI features section** — Purple + tech blue (innovation, creativity)
- **Luxury badges** — Gold/amber accents (premium feel)

## Accessibility Rules

- **WCAG AA:** 4.5:1 contrast ratio for normal text, 3:1 for large text (18px+ bold / 24px+ regular)
- **WCAG AAA:** 7:1 contrast for normal, 4.5:1 for large (gold standard)
- **79.1% of homepages have low-contrast text** — mat karo ye galti
- **Use color + icon/pattern** dono — colorblind users ke liye (1 in 12 men)
- Color blindness types: Deuteranopia (green-weak), Protanopia (red-weak), Tritanopia (blue-weak)

2. **Cool & Calm** — blues, greens, teals (tech, health, finance)
3. **Earthy & Natural** — browns, olives, rusts (organic, sustainability)
4. **Bold & Vibrant** — neon + high saturation (creative, youth brands)
5. **Pastel & Soft** — muted, light tones (wellness, baby, lifestyle)

7. **Monochrome & Minimal** — single hue variations (modern, professional)

## Figma Tips

- **Color Variables** use karo — ek jagah change karo, sab jagah reflect ho
- **Styles panel** se reusable color styles banao

- **Color wheel** se harmonious schemes explore karo
- **Community templates** — pre-made palettes as starting point
