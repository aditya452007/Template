## 13 Visual Trends Defining 2026

1. **3D/AR Immersion** — WebGL se product spin, virtual tours, try-before-you-buy
   - *Examples: Nike, IKEA (virtual room visualization)*
   
2. **Experimental Navigation** — Radial menus, hidden drawers, interactive maps
   - *Examples: Locomotive, The Outline, Google Arts & Culture*

3. **Bold Saturated Colors** — Neon gradients, high-contrast, "dopamine design"
   - *Examples: Lush, Headspace, Starface*

4. **Typography as Storytelling** — Custom fonts, oversized headlines, variable fonts
   - *Examples: Glossier, Samsung, Spotify Wrapped*

5. **Dark Mode Default** — Reduce eye strain, save OLED battery
   - *Examples: YouTube, X, Slack, VS Code*

6. **Micro-animations** — Scroll triggers, button ripples, smart animate
   - *Must enhance journey, not slow performance*

7. **Gamification** — Points, levels, badges, progress bars
   - *Examples: Duolingo (streaks), Sephora (rewards)*

8. **Neumorphism** — Soft shadows + subtle gradients (blend of skeuomorphic + minimal)
   - *Examples: Apple, Flow Ninja, Stripe*

9. **Retrofuturism** — Neon accents, chrome textures, pixel art, sci-fi
   - *Nostalgia + future vibes*

10. **Maximalism** — Rich colors, overlapping visuals, bold fonts, dense compositions
    - *Examples: Spotify, Liquid Death*

11. **Collage Style** — Layered stickers, torn textures, cutout photos
    - *Scrapbook creativity — youth brands*

12. **Neo-Brutalism / Anti-Design** — Raw, unpolished, intentionally "ugly"
    - *Examples: Balenciaga, Diesel, Mailchimp*

13. **Sustainable Design** — Optimize page weight, green hosting, minimal energy
    - *Growing expectation, not just trend*

## AI Trends in Web Design 2026

- **68% of developers use AI to generate code** (Figma AI Report 2025)
- **Twice as many teams built agentic products in 2025 vs 2024**
- AI interfaces shifting from "prompt box" → intuitive, visual AI experiences
- AI handles mechanical layout (8px grid math, auto-layout, backdrop blurs)
- Designers spend more time on brand/refinement, less on repetitive layout

## What's OUT in 2026

- ❌ Generic "prompt box" AI interface
- ❌ Heavy JS bundles without SSR
- ❌ Image carousels (consistently test poorly in usability)
- ❌ Skeleton screens everywhere (edge computing eliminates need)
- ❌ Static dark/light mode without user preference detection
- ❌ Stock photos where custom product imagery is possible
